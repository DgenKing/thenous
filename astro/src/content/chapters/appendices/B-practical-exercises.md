**Exercise 1: Present-Moment Coherence Training**
- **Duration:** 5-10 minutes daily
- Sit comfortably, close eyes
- Say internally: "This is not the past, not the future, this is now"
- Feel yourself drop into present moment
- Notice when mind wanders to past/future
- Gently return: "This is now"
- Notice any physical sensations (waves, pressure, warmth)
- These indicate increasing coherence
- Practice daily, consistency matters most
- **Track:** Record any insights, synchronicities, or accurate intuitions that follow practice.

**Exercise 2: Synchronicity Documentation**
- **Duration:** Ongoing
- Keep daily log of "coincidences"
- Note: date, description, feeling about it
- Don't dismiss any as "just coincidence"
- Look for patterns over weeks/months
- Notice if frequency increases with practice
- Test following vs ignoring them
- Document outcomes
- This builds awareness of network guidance already present.

**Exercise 3: Intuitive Accuracy Testing**
- **Method:**
  - Before researching something, write down your intuitive sense
  - Rate confidence (1-10)
  - Then research and verify
  - Track accuracy over time
  - Notice if accuracy improves with practice
  - Correlate with your coherence state when intuiting
- This quantifies network access development.

**Exercise 4: Thought Depth Awareness**
- **Practice:**
  - Throughout day, notice quality of thinking
  - Label: Scattered (washing machine), Focused, Deep, Profound
  - Notice which produces insights
  - Notice physical sensations at each level
  - Practice moving toward deeper states
  - Observe effects on life
- This trains awareness of entanglement levels.

**Exercise 5: Group Coherence Experiment**
- **With Friends:**
  - Gather group for meditation
  - Synchronize to same focus (breath, question, intention)
  - Sit together 20-30 minutes
  - Share experiences after
  - Notice if insights came simultaneously
  - Document collective experiences
  - Compare to individual practice
- Tests if group coherence amplifies effects.
