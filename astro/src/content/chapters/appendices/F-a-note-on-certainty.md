**Intellectual Honesty Requires Acknowledging:**

**What We Know:**
- Quantum effects exist in brain microtubules (proven)
- Consciousness correlates with microtubule function (proven)
- Simultaneous discoveries are documented (proven)
- Global Consciousness Project shows effects (proven)
- Meditation enhances consciousness (proven)

**What We Theorize:**
- Thought depth determines entanglement strength (logical extension)
- Entanglement enables information transfer between brains (proposed mechanism)
- 8 billion brains form coherent network (extrapolation)
- This explains all simultaneous discoveries (explanatory framework)

**What Remains Unknown:**
- Exact mechanism of inter-brain entanglement (needs discovery)
- Precise relationship between thought depth and quantum coherence (needs measurement)
- Whether this is THE explanation or ONE explanation (needs testing)
- Full implications and capabilities (needs exploration)

**The Appropriate Stance:**
- Open-minded but critical
- Enthusiastic but honest
- Willing to test rigorously
- Ready to update based on evidence
- Comfortable with uncertainty
- Committed to truth over belief

This theory is offered as:
- A comprehensive framework (not dogma)
- A testable hypothesis (not absolute truth)
- An invitation to investigate (not demand to believe)
- A contribution to understanding (not final answer)

May it serve the collective awakening while maintaining intellectual integrity.
