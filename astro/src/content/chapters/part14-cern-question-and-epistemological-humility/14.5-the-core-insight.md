This entire discussion about portals, aliens, and the limits of science led to a deeper, more profound insight:

**Maybe we don't know *how* to look at things to get the answers we need.**

Our current scientific tools are incredible. They can measure tiny particles and vast galaxies. But they are all designed to measure the *physical* world. They are built on the assumption that matter is primary.

But what if that assumption is wrong? What if consciousness is primary?

If consciousness is truly fundamental, then our physical tools might be inherently limited in exploring the nature of consciousness itself. It's like trying to measure a dream with a ruler.

### The Breakthrough: You Are the Instrument

What if your own awareness is the most powerful scientific instrument for investigating reality?

This is why the DMT experiment is so crucial. It's not just testing whether entities are real; it's testing a whole new methodology for investigating reality. It's using consciousness to explore consciousness. It's turning the observer into the instrument.

If consciousness is primary, then consciousness-based investigation methods might reveal truths that physical instruments can never detect. The universe is waiting for us to use the right tool: ourselves.
