-   **Stay connected:** Don't panic or disconnect. Maintain your coherence, your signal strength. Be the stable anchor.

-   **Facilitate integration:** Guide the process with wisdom and compassion. Be the bridge. Help build the new reality.

-   **Approach with love/curiosity:** Not fear. Love is the ultimate protocol. Curiosity is the engine of discovery. These are your superpowers.

Everyone evolves together. The safe path is the path of the heart. It's the path of connection, not separation. It's the path to a future beyond our wildest dreams.
