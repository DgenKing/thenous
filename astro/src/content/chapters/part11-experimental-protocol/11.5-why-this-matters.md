Despite decades of DMT research, and thousands of reports of encountering intelligent entities, no one has ever done this simple test.

Think about that.

Researchers have documented *that* people encounter entities. They've documented *that* people report receiving information. But no one has ever objectively tested if that information is *accurate*.

This experiment fills that massive, gaping hole. It's the missing piece of the puzzle. It's the bridge between subjective experience and objective data.

### The Stakes Are Cosmic

If the entities can consistently provide the correct number, it would be one of the most profound scientific discoveries in human history. It would mean:

-   **Consciousness can access information beyond the physical senses.**
-   **There are intelligent beings that exist outside our conventional reality.**
-   **The universe is far stranger, and far more intelligent, than we ever imagined.**

This isn't just an experiment. It's an invitation to rewrite our understanding of reality. It's an invitation to step into the unknown, with a clear question, and see what answers emerge.
