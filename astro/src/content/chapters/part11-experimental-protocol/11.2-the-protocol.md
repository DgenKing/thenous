This isn't a casual trip. This is a scientific experiment. Every step is designed to eliminate bias and get a clear, objective answer to the biggest question in consciousness research.

### What You'll Need:

-   20 identical pieces of paper
-   A pen
-   An envelope or sealed container
-   A video camera (your phone is fine)
-   DMT (only where legal and safe)

### Step 1: The Setup (Before the Experience)

1.  **Prepare the Numbers:** Write the numbers 1 through 20 on separate pieces of identical paper. Fold each paper identically so they are indistinguishable.

2.  **Random Selection:** Mix the folded papers thoroughly. Close your eyes and randomly select one. *Without looking at the number*, immediately place it into the envelope and seal it. Place the sealed envelope in a drawer or another secure, private location.

3.  **Record It:** Video record this entire process. This proves that you genuinely don't know the number.

### Step 2: Mental Preparation (Before DMT)

1.  **Set the Intention:** Spend 5-10 minutes mentally preparing. Repeat to yourself: "I will remember to ask the question." Visualize yourself clearly asking the entities for the number.

2.  **Write a Reminder:** Write "ASK: What number?" on your hand or arm as a physical reminder. Set a mental anchor: "When I arrive, I ask immediately."

### Step 3: During the Experience

1.  **Ask Clearly:** As soon as you encounter the entities or feel you are in the "space," ask clearly and directly: **"What number is written on the paper in the drawer?"**

2.  **Receive the Answer:** Accept the first clear answer you receive without second-guessing. Note *how* they communicate it (telepathically, visually, through direct knowing, etc.). If they don't respond, note that too.

### Step 4: Immediately After

1.  **Document:** Before doing anything else, write down the number you received. Rate your confidence level (1-10). Document the experience in as much detail as possible.

2.  **DO NOT Open the Envelope Yet.**

### Step 5: Verification (The Reveal)

1.  **Record the Reveal:** Later, video record yourself opening the sealed envelope. Show the timestamp on the video.

2.  **State the Result:** Clearly reveal the paper and state the result: "The number was [X], and I received [Y]. Correct/Incorrect."

This is how we get objective data from a subjective experience. This is how we turn a mystical encounter into hard science.
