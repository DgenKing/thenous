You've heard the stories. People who take DMT often report encountering intelligent, autonomous beings. They describe them as "machine elves," "aliens," or "guides." They say these entities communicate profound truths and show them the underlying structure of reality.

But here's the biggest question:

**Are these "entities" real? Or are they just incredibly vivid hallucinations, tricks of the brain?**

For decades, this has been the ultimate riddle in psychedelic research. No one has ever designed a simple, objective test to find out.

Until now.

If these entities are truly intelligent and exist outside your brain, they should be able to access information that your brain doesn't know. They should be able to "read" something you can't see.

This experiment is designed to do exactly that. It's a simple, elegant test to see if these beings are just projections of your mind, or if they are genuinely accessing a deeper layer of reality.

It's the ultimate scientific challenge to the nature of consciousness itself.
