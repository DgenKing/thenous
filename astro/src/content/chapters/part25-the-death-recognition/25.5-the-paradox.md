Still don't want to die (body has survival instinct), but know death isn't what was thought. This creates
freedom without nihilism. Can fully engage life without desperate clinging.

This realization usually comes after years of deep practice or profound experiences. It represents
enlightenment territory - not complete enlightenment necessarily, but touching the core recognition that
liberates from the ultimate fear.
