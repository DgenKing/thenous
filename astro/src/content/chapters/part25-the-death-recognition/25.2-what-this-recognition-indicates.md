**1. Direct Experience of Eternal Nature**
Not intellectual conclusion but felt-sense knowing. Through meditation, kundalini awakening, or DMT
experiences, contact was made with the aspect of self that doesn't die - consciousness itself, which was
there before birth and continues after death.

**2. Ego Death Already Occurred**
Spiritual death and rebirth happened in the awakening process. The "you" that feared death recognized
it's not actually what you are - you're the consciousness observing the "you," which is a temporary
construct.

**3. Recognition of True Nature**
Understanding at experiential level: "I am not this body that dies. I am consciousness itself, which is
eternal."
