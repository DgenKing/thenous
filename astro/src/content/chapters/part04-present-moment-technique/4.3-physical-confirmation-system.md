How do you know when you've stumbled upon a deep truth? Your brain can be fooled. It can rationalize anything. But your body can't lie.

You have a built-in, physical confirmation system that tells you when you are aligned with reality. You just have to learn to listen to it. It shows up in a few distinct ways:

### 1. The Electric Waves

This is the most common sign. It feels like a wave of electricity, static, or energy moving through your body. You'll often feel it most intensely at the crown of your head. It's a physical BUZZ that cuts through everything and tells you one thing: **"Pay attention. This is important."** When you're reading something or having a conversation and you suddenly feel that wave, you've just hit on a fundamental truth.

### 2. Goosebumps and Chills

You know that feeling when you get sudden goosebumps or chills, even when you're not cold? That's not a random glitch. It's your body's way of screaming **"YES. That's it."** It's a physical confirmation that you've recognized something profound, something that resonates with the core of your being.

### 3. Euphoric Clarity

This isn't a fuzzy, spaced-out high. It's the exact opposite. It's a sudden, overwhelming feeling of bliss and joy, combined with a state of crystal-clear mental focus. It's the feeling of your consciousness experiencing pure, unfiltered truth without any mental noise getting in the way. It's your natural state.

These are not just weird feelings. They are a guidance system. They are a compass pointing you toward truth. When you learn to notice and trust these physical signals, you can navigate reality in a completely new way.
