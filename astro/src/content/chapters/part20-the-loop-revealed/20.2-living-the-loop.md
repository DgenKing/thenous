After the initial breakthroughs, the excitement, the mind-blowing insights, came the hard part: living the loop. It's a constant feeling of questioning everything, a nagging sense that something is fundamentally "off" with reality.

### The Experience:

-   **Before:** Life was simple. Manageable. Certain. You were asleep, but you were comfortable in the dream. The world made sense.

-   **Now:** That comfort is gone. You have doubt on reality itself. Questions all the time. The old certainties are shattered, and the new ones haven't formed yet. It's like the ground beneath your feet has disappeared.

-   **The Feeling:** It's disorienting. It's exhausting. It's the uncomfortable middle. It's the feeling that you're going crazy, but you're not. You're just waking up.

It's like being stuck in a movie where the plot keeps changing, and you're not sure who the main character is anymore. You're constantly searching for solid ground, but every answer leads to another question.

This isn't a sign of failure. It's a sign of progress. You're not going crazy. You're waking up. And the loop is just part of the process.
