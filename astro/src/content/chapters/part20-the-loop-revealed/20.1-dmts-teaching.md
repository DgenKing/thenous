Remember the DMT vision? The one that showed a "loop"—the cyclical nature of awakening, questioning, and consciousness exploring itself? That wasn't just a vision. It was a prophetic map of the journey to come.

### The Cycle of Awakening:

The loop is the process of:

-   **Opening:** A new insight, a new connection, a glimpse behind the curtain.
-   **Questioning:** Everything you thought you knew. The old certainties shatter.
-   **Disorientation:** Feeling lost, nothing feels solid, reality seems "off."
-   **Seeking Answers:** Diving deeper, looking for clarity, trying to make sense of it all.
-   **More Questions:** The answers only lead to deeper, more profound questions.
-   **Opening Further:** The cycle repeats, each time dissolving more certainty, each time going deeper.

This loop continues... until what? Until you find a new ground, a new way of being that can hold the expanded reality.

DMT showed the pattern. Now, it was time to live it.
