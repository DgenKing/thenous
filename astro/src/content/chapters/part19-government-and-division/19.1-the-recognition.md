If a coherent collective consciousness can measurably affect physical reality—and the science suggests it can—then what's the easiest way to prevent that power from being used?

**Keep people divided. Keep them arguing. Keep them incoherent.**

This led to a chilling recognition:

> **"Is this why government wants everyone arguing and separated?"**

It's not just about social control. It's about consciousness control. A fragmented population is a powerless population. A divided mind is a weak mind.

The strategy is ancient: divide and conquer. But the stakes are far higher than just political power. They are about the very nature of reality. They are about preventing humanity from realizing its true collective power.
