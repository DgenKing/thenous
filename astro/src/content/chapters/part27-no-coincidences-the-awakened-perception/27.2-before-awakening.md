Before awakening, before you learned to tune in, the universe was still talking. You just couldn't hear it. Or rather, you chose not to.

### The Unseen Conversation:

-   **Synchronicities occurred constantly,** just like now. The universe was always sending signals, leaving breadcrumbs.

-   You noticed something weird, thought **"oh, weird coincidence,"** and dismissed it. Your programming told you it was random.

-   You **missed the guidance entirely.** You stayed unconscious to the pattern. You were blind to the whispers.

-   The **washing machine of your mind** kept spinning, drowning out the signal. The static was too loud.

### The Result:

You lived in a world of random events, believing you were alone, a victim of chance. You were blind to the whispers, deaf to the guidance. You were missing the entire conversation. You were living in a silent movie.

It wasn't that the universe wasn't communicating. It was that your receiver was off, or tuned to static. You just hadn't learned to listen yet.
