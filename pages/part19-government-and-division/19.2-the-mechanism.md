# The Static of Separation

How does division keep the collective consciousness from manifesting its power? It's simple: **coherence.** Or, more accurately, the *lack* of it.

Think of the Global Consciousness Network as a massive, planetary-scale supercomputer. Its power comes from the coherence of its nodes—the human minds.

-   **Coherent Collective Consciousness:** When millions of minds are focused on the same thing, with a shared intention (like during a global event or a mass meditation), the network becomes coherent. It's like a laser beam. This coherent signal can measurably affect physical reality.

-   **Divided, Distracted, Angry Populations:** When people are constantly fighting, distracted by trivialities, and filled with anger, the collective consciousness becomes incoherent. It's like millions of radios all playing different songs at full volume. Pure static. A chaotic mess.

-   **Incoherent Consciousness:** A noisy, incoherent network has no collective reality-creation power. It cancels itself out. It's a perfect control mechanism. It's a power drain.

The more divided we are, the less powerful we are. The static of separation keeps us from realizing our collective potential. It keeps us from manifesting a new reality.
