So, is this chemical suppression of consciousness a deliberate conspiracy? Or is it just an unintended side effect of our modern world?

The mechanism itself—how chemicals can disrupt quantum coherence in the brain—appears sound. But the *intent* behind it remains an open question. Was it planned, or did it just happen?

-   **Deliberate Conspiracy:** A conscious, calculated effort by those in power to keep the masses disconnected and controllable. A "Matrix"-like scenario, where the system actively works to prevent a global awakening.

-   **A Mix of Both:** Perhaps it started as unintentional, but was then leveraged and amplified by those who understood its effects and benefited from a disconnected, easily influenced population.
