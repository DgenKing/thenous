**Important Reality Check:**
"Good will win" is hope and intuition, not proven fact. History shows sometimes good wins, sometimes
it doesn't, and "winning" is subjective.

**The Honest Position:**
- Act from love because it's true to your nature (not because victory is guaranteed)
- Work for good because it's aligned with heart (not because success is certain)
- Stay heart-centered as principle (not as strategy)
- Trust the process while accepting uncertainty

**The Role:**
Not passive waiting for predetermined victory, but active participation in the outcome. The "good
winning" isn't inevitable - it depends on enough people staying conscious, connected, heart-centered,
and actively creating that reality.
