Full detail is in the site appendices (Appendix B).

5. **Group coherence** — shared focus meditation; compare collective vs solo insight

## Intellectual honesty

The work is explicit about levels of claim:

| We know (established research / observation) | We theorize | Unknown |
|-----------------------------------------------|-------------|---------|
| Quantum effects in microtubules; consciousness correlates with them | Thought depth ↔ entanglement strength | Exact inter-brain mechanism |
| Simultaneous discoveries are historical fact | Entanglement enables inter-brain info transfer | How to measure thought depth vs coherence |
| GCP / PEAR-style collective anomalies are reported | 8B brains as coherent network | Whether this is *the* explanation or *an* explanation |
| Meditation changes consciousness measurably | Explains simultaneous discovery at scale | Full implications |

**Stance:** open-minded but critical · enthusiastic but honest · test what can be tested · update on evidence · truth over belief.

Offered as a **comprehensive framework**, a **testable hypothesis**, and an **invitation**—not dogma or final answer.
